Our History

The Palm & Pine named in honor of an ancient date that offers some mesmerizing memories. The 1599 AD restaurant serves as a perfect blend of traditional and contemporary Indian cuisine that attracts most of the guests. The place is ideal for romantic couples that offer couples a serene atmosphere. Explore its stunning garden area, where you can enjoy continental, Indian, and Thai dishes.

The Palm & Pine serves as a casual dining restaurant that takes care of your hunger as late as 1 am. Enjoy delicious North Indian cuisines that make you attracted to the restaurant.

The restaurant is entirely praised for its stylish and exotic presentation that has become a nice joint for tourists, especially food lovers. Visit the place and satisfy your hunger along with hygienic and tasty food at an affordable price.
